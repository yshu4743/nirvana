What we do

We produce and export rare, non-hybrid ice apple variants to the USA, Singapore,the Middle East and Malaysia catering exclusively to premium hotels, hospitals, and elite communities. Our 100% natural, chemical-free products focus on pure, restorative nutrition and the traditional health benefits of this rare fruit to support disease prevention and holistic wellness.

Purity & Sourcing

We use only rare, non-hybrid, and wild-harvested ice apples. No artificial additives, preservatives, or GMOs are used in any stage of production.

Targeted Distribution

Direct supply chain to 5-star resorts, luxury wellness retreats, high-end private hospitals, and ultra-wealthy residential sectors.

Health-First Philosophy

Positioned as a "super-fruit" with medicinal properties, focusing on hydration, digestive health, and natural cooling benefits for the body.

Premium Presentation

Luxury-grade, eco-friendly packaging designed to maintain the fruit's integrity while appealing to high-net-worth aesthetic standards.

Genetic Integrity

Our fruits come from traditional palm lineages that have remained unchanged for centuries, offering the original taste and texture intended by nature.

Limited Availability

Because these are wild-harvested and not mass-produced hybrids, each batch is a rare, seasonal luxury reserved for the most discerning consumers.
